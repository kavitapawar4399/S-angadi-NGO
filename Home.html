About S Angdi-NGO

S Angdi is an NGO that is involved in promoting the welfare of the economically backward sections of the society by generating employment across various sectors. It was founded in 2009 by a team of like-minded individuals with a commitment to eradicate poverty. Our constant efforts have touched the lives of people. We believe in community development. Urbanization has had an impact on the traditional economic systems in the country and has shifted the socioeconomic preferences.
The socioeconomic growth and wellbeing of people are so interconnected, that it is not feasible to ignore any one of them.
